Terms and Conditions

Definitions and Parties

  • Vendor: Any individual, group, or business that completes the registration form and submits payment to participate in the Edmonton Multi Cultural Bazaar.
  • Organizer: Unity Celebrations, specifically the Event Management Team responsible for coordinating the Edmonton Multi Cultural Bazaar.

Registration and Payment

  • Vendor spaces are confirmed only after both registration form completion and full payment receipt.
  • E-transfer payments must be sent to celebrationsofunity@gmail.com with your business name in the message field.
  • Vendors must include their business name when making payment to ensure proper allocation.
  • Registration fees are non-refundable if cancellation occurs within 5 days of the event.

Vendor Spaces and Setup

  • Each vendor will be assigned a designated space based on registration type and availability.
  • Clothing vendors are permitted one display rack, which will receive an identifying number during setup.
  • Setup times: Saturday (July 26) - 8:00 AM to 9:30 AM; Sunday (July 27) - 8:30 AM to 9:30 AM.
  • Vendors must be fully set up and ready for business by the event opening time.
  • Vendors are responsible for their own tables, chairs, and display materials unless otherwise arranged with organizers.

Vendor Conduct and Responsibilities

  • Vendors must maintain their spaces in a clean, safe, and professional manner throughout the event.
  • Vendors are expected to remain open during all advertised event hours unless otherwise approved by organizers.
  • Vendors are responsible for their own cash handling, change, and sales transactions.
  • Vendors must comply with all applicable laws and regulations related to their business operations.
  • Vendors and one helper are permitted per registration; additional assistants require prior approval.

Product and Service Guidelines

  • All products and services must be as described in the vendor application.
  • Organizers reserve the right to request removal of inappropriate or misrepresented items.
  • Vendors are solely responsible for the quality and safety of their products and services.
  • Unity Celebrations and event organizers assume no liability for products sold or services provided.

Special Guidelines for Food Vendors

Licensing and Certification

  • All food vendors must possess valid food handler's permits or food safety certification.
  • Certificates must be available for inspection upon request.

Sanitation and Hygiene

  • Proper handwashing facilities must be maintained.
  • Food handlers must wear appropriate hair restraints and gloves.
  • Regular sanitizing of food contact surfaces is required.

Food Storage and Handling

  • Cold foods must be kept below 4°C (40°F).
  • Hot foods must be maintained above 60°C (140°F).
  • Proper separation of raw and ready-to-eat foods is mandatory.
  • All food items must be properly labeled and dated.

Special Guidelines for Henna Artists

  • Only natural henna products are permitted at this event.
  • Black henna or products containing PPD (para-phenylenediamine) are strictly prohibited.
  • Vendors may be asked to show their ingredients for verification.
  • Any vendor found using chemical henna will be immediately removed from the event without refund.
  • Vendors must display appropriate allergy warnings and offer patch tests upon request.

Liability and Insurance

  • Vendors operate at their own risk and are responsible for their own insurance coverage.
  • Unity Celebrations and event organizers are not responsible for any damage, theft, or loss of vendor property.
  • Vendors assume all liability for any harm caused by their products, services, displays, or personal actions.
  • By participating, vendors agree to indemnify and hold harmless Unity Celebrations and all event organizers from any claims arising from vendor activities.

Event Management Rights

  • Organizers reserve the right to accept or reject any vendor application.
  • Organizers will determine all vendor space allocations.
  • Organizers reserve the right to ask any vendor to leave for non-compliance with these terms or for conduct deemed detrimental to the event.
  • Repeat vendors from previous events may be given preference in space allocation.
